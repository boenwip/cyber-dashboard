AI Guide

Practical prompts for everyday work tasks, what you should never share with AI tools, and how to use AI safely in your organisation.

⚠ What Not to Share with AI

AI tools like ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Copilot send your input to external servers. Treat them like a public forum unless you're on an enterprise-licensed, data-isolated plan.

✕
Never enter client or student personal information
Names, contact details, enrolment records, health information, Tax File Numbers — all protected under the Privacy Act and your organisation's obligations.
✕
Never paste confidential contracts, financial data, or board documents
These may be used to train future models, stored by the provider, or exposed in a breach. Use internal tools for sensitive documents.
✕
Never share credentials, API keys, or system details
Passwords, API tokens, internal IP addresses, and system architecture details should never appear in an AI prompt.
✕
Never share unreleased product plans, M&A activity, or sensitive strategy
Commercially sensitive information — especially anything price-sensitive for listed companies — must stay internal.
~
Be cautious with internal processes and org structure
Detailed org charts, system architecture, and security policies could help an attacker target your organisation if exposed.

AI Tools in Your Stack

Claude (Anthropic)
Strong at long documents, analysis, coding, and nuanced writing. Data not used for training by default on claude.ai. Enterprise plan available for data isolation.
🟢 Safer for work use (no training on your data)
ChatGPT (OpenAI)
Most widely used. Free tier may use your conversations for training — check your settings. ChatGPT Team and Enterprise plans are data-isolated.
🟡 Check your data settings before sharing work content
Microsoft Copilot
Integrated into Microsoft 365. If your organisation has Microsoft 365 E3/E5 with Copilot, your data stays within your Microsoft tenant.
🟢 Safe for org-licensed users within tenant boundary
Google Gemini
Integrated into Google Workspace. Workspace Business and Enterprise plans include data protection. Personal Google accounts may use data for improvement.
🟡 Safe on Workspace Business+ — check personal account settings
Canva AI Features
Magic Write, AI image generation, and other features. Do not upload images or text containing client or personal information.
🟡 Safe for public-facing design work, not sensitive content
Zoom AI Companion
Summarises meetings and generates action items. Check your organisation's Zoom admin settings — meeting data should not leave your tenant.
🟢 Safe when enabled and configured by org admin
