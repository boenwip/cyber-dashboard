Resources & Tools

Practical tools for Australians — check if your data has been breached, learn from the best, and connect with the right organisations.

Data Breach Checker

Check if your email address has appeared in a known data breach. Powered by HaveIBeenPwned, run by Australian security researcher Troy Hunt.

🔒 Your email is sent directly to the HaveIBeenPwned API and is not stored, logged, or shared by pseudosec. The check is read-only. HIBP Privacy Policy ↗

Australian Signals Directorate's reporting portal for cyber incidents affecting Australian organisations or individuals.
cyber.gov.au/report ↗

ACCC's ScamWatch collects reports from Australians. Your report helps protect others and informs national scam statistics.
scamwatch.gov.au ↗

Australia and New Zealand's national identity & cyber support service. Free case managers help you respond to identity theft.

24/7 hotline for Australians needing cyber security assistance. Free, confidential, government-run.

Security Tools

🔍
HaveIBeenPwned
Check if your email or phone number has appeared in a data breach. Free, run by Australian Troy Hunt (QLD). Trusted globally.
Free · Australian-run
🔬
VirusTotal
Analyse suspicious files, URLs, and email attachments using 70+ antivirus engines simultaneously. Free for basic use.
Free
🕵
OSINT Framework
Interactive map of open-source intelligence tools. Useful for understanding what information about you or your organisation is publicly visible.
Free
📋
CISA KEV Catalog
CISA's Known Exploited Vulnerabilities catalog — the definitive list of vulnerabilities being actively used in attacks right now.
Free · Authoritative
🌐
Shodan
Search engine for internet-connected devices. Security professionals use it to understand what their organisation exposes to the internet.
Free basic · Intermediate

Check email security configuration — SPF, DKIM, DMARC. Find out if your domain is properly protected against email spoofing.
Free
